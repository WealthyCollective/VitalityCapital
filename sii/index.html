Territory Analysis

Can We Get Uniqlo?

Current Status
  • Al-Futtaim Group operates Uniqlo in UAE (Dubai Mall, Dubai Hills, Mall of Emirates) via JV with Fast Retailing.
  • Al-Futtaim already operates IKEA and M&S in Qatar — strongly suggesting they hold broader GCC territory rights.
  • A @uniqlo_qa Instagram account exists, indicating Qatar entry is already planned under Al-Futtaim.
  • Fast Retailing does NOT franchise. They only enter JV/partnership agreements with large retail operators.
Assessment: Low Probability

Direct Uniqlo deal unlikely. Al-Futtaim almost certainly holds Qatar exclusivity. However, two alternative paths exist:

GU (Fast Retailing)

Uniqlo's trendier, lower-price sister brand. NOT in GCC. May be separately licensable — genuine opportunity.

Sub-Licence Model

Explore whether SIIL could operate Uniqlo stores under Al-Futtaim's master licence.

Redirect Strategy

Focus on genuinely available white-space brands in F&B and lifestyle where SIIL has a clear first-mover advantage.
