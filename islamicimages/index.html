The Founder
Peter Sanders
A Lifetime Passion

What began as a young photographer's journey through the counterculture of 1960s London became a lifelong devotion to capturing the beauty, depth, and diversity of the Muslim world. Over five decades and 30+ countries, Peter Sanders built one of the most significant photographic archives in Islamic history — driven not by commerce, but by love.

Recognised among the 500 most influential Muslims globally, a permanent Ambassador for MOSAIC (founded by HM King Charles), and the subject of a 2024 retrospective at Ithra Gallery that drew over 300,000 visitors — Peter now turns that passion into a platform that will preserve this heritage and serve the Ummah for generations.
