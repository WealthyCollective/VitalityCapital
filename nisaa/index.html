Cohort I · Portfolio Focus

COHORT I:
SIX VERTICALS.

All six pilotable inside Fauji Foundation's network of 11 hospitals — today. No other studio on earth can say the same.

01
Maternal & Prenatal Health
AI-powered prenatal screening, remote monitoring & postnatal support for Pakistan's 4M+ annual births.
02
Women's Oncology
Breast & cervical cancer early detection — building on FF's active oncology & mammography programs.
03
Reproductive Health Tech
Fertility, PCOS, endometriosis diagnostics with culturally-contextualised digital care pathways.
04
Women's Mental Health
Maternal mental health, postpartum depression — massively unaddressed in Pakistan's clinical settings.
05
Chronic Disease in Women
Diabetes, thyroid, hypertension — presenting differently in women, poorly served by current tools.
06
Digital Health Access
Telemedicine, AI symptom-checkers and health literacy in Urdu for semi-urban Pakistani women.
