By converting 22 small hotel rooms into 11 grand performance suites, each stay becomes a fully private residence with dedicated treatment space, personal recovery equipment, and concierge clinical team. This isn't a hotel room rate — it's an all-inclusive performance residency. Each week includes full sports performance testing, daily IV and recovery protocols, bespoke nutrition programming, unlimited clinical access, and private suite accommodation. For a Premier League footballer earning £300K+ per week, this is a career-extending investment. For their clubs, it's insurance on a £100M asset.
