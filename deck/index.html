Investment Thesis

The Dirty Pipes Hypothesis

"Modern chronic disease originates in metabolic dysfunction — seeded by industrial food systems, sedentary lifestyles, and environmental toxins. Intercepting disease upstream delivers better outcomes at lower cost."
The future is not treating illness faster — it's preventing decline at the root. Healthcare is moving upstream, from treatment to prediction, optimisation, and longevity. AI enables continuous personalisation, turning health from episodic care into an always-on prevention system. But AI demands energy at a scale the world has never seen. Energy is the foundational dependency. Without clean, abundant power, the AI revolution — and every healthcare platform it enables — stalls. We invest across the full continuum: from early-stage foodtech and preventive medtech, through AI diagnostics and clinical platforms, to the energy infrastructure powering it all.
